Why do you want to become an Admin? I've been saying for 3 seasons now that I'm going to become an admin, and its finally time. I want to become an admin because I believe that this league should continue to be a safehaven for counter strike players who want to compete, even if they are not the best player. I believe that CSC continues to lift NACS and I want to help it continue to lift NA (and Europe ig if that turns out to be a thing) even further.
10:19 PM
What prior experience do you have that you believe will help you as an Admin? VDC Season 1 Admin (also head mod in VDC), 3 season GM, Former Events Head, readtldr.gg podcast, guy people know from streams (wow my resume looks really short when i put it like that)
10:19 PM
What things do you hope to implement, reinforce, or remove as an Admin. I hope to make CSC a place that people feel welcomed regardless of their skill level, and to help continue to be a driving force behind different CSC projects, including media and reapplying my efforts into events. My main thing, though, is to keep the ship running and communicate to everyone better then before. After CSC went into a season that caused many GM's to lose a team (mine included), I want to continue to make sure that we keep the players we have, and gain new players. I hope to take the beneficial things from VDC that I helped implement and bring them to CSC like transparency between the leadership and everyone else. A problem that I have found in CSC is the lack of transparency between everyone, it feels like admins have stuff in their heads, and teams have some stuff and things don't get communicated to others, including the normal players, some of whom need to know these thing.
10:19 PM
Anything else you would like to add? Well... there's the elephant in the room. VDC. Yes, I went and helped to make VDC and held admin there for a season, and was definitely absent in CSC for a time. But, I think that going to VDC showed me a lot of how to run a league and how to deal with the day to day of being an admin. And as of today (8/8), I stepped down as owner/admin/leadership in VDC to focus on CSC and helping CSC become the best version of itself it can be. (edited)
