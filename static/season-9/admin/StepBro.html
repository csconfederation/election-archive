What things do you hope to implement, reinforce, or remove as an Admin. I am not gonna sit here and promise the community some long list of things that I will implement as admin. Number one: structure for all future seasons to come. This is probably the most important thing to me and something that CSC fails at. At the core CSC is a for fun league and its matches are Tuesday and Thursday. This basic idea starts to get bogged down by an admin team who is cluttered and disorganized, not knowing when to release certain posts and not able to hit basic deadlines. That is absolutely detrimental to everyone in CSC. If admins are tripping over their own heels not knowing what is coming next and don't have the basics in order they are unable to work on the actual big changes in CSC. The implementation here is to work on a set calendar/guide for CSC that can be used for many seasons to come. This includes pretty much all things such as dates and even when things like activity check, or miniscule changes like when commands need to be changed. This was my idea and something I ran on back in Season 7 and was wanting to implement that I unfortunately wasn't able to due to not winning re-election.
