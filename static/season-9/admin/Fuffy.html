What things do you hope to implement, reinforce, or remove as an Admin. As an Admin I understand the behind the scenes processes that go into making CSC the fun and welcoming experience we love. I've seen first hand the roadblocks that we must overcome to continue operating. One such issue is the inconsistencies in regular tasks. My goal is to create an Admin Handbook of tasks so that season over season can be easily passed down. When I started out in S7 it was a struggle given the lack of organized training. Moving forward the Admin Handbook could be used to keep things consistent and quick each season. The automation of signups (/signup) is the improvement I most pushed for between S7-S8, and it has drastically reduced one of the biggest time syncs of the Admins. It has also opened up the possibility of better contracts, stats pages, and more. I will continue to assist Tech in getting these tools to better CSC. Season over season we always change our player numbers, but this most recent season was unique in that we dropped down and had to remove some teams. Having worked on advertising for three seasons (including creating the video that helped blow us up in S7) I am familiar with the process of growing our player numbers. The goal going forwards will be to maintain our current player base, given the regular turn over between seasons. Healthy growth will help us maintain the teams and tiers we love!
