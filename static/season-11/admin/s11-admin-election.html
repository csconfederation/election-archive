Hello all! Welcome to the Season 11 Admin Election Discussions. This channel (and threads) will be made available until 11:59PM ET on Wednesday (4/12). Voting will open shortly after or the next day. There are three admin positions open (Mecha, Andrew, and Cash are all up for reelection). The backer pool consisted of 18 Former Admins, General Managers (GM), and Committee Heads (CH). Anexilia (GM), Assass1n (GM), Crazy (CH - Events), Fuffy (Former Admin/CH - Media), Hawkish (GM), Hermit (GM), M3CHATR0NIC (Former Admin), megatallicayer (CH - Mods), mutant (CH - TM), Nightfury (CH - Tech), raptor (Former Admin), Saunce (GM), Spence (Former Admin/GM), Splatmonkey (Former Admin), Supreme (GM), TNoodle (Former Admin/GM), VIPVeral (Former Admin), and yetiF (CH - Stats). We had 6 candidates backed by the pool, they are:
  • andrew
  • Anonimouse
  • ComradSniper
  • Homer
  • Jermz
  • owen Please use the threads to ask candidates questions, even if it is the same question for each candidate. This helps to keep things organized as well as makes it much easier for each of our candidates to be able to keep track of what questions they have being asked to them. As a reminder, you must have participated in Season 10 as a player (signed, FA, PFA) or staff member to be eligible to vote, but anyone is eligible to participate in asking questions to the candidates. Please keep these channels on topic and do not post memes or gifs. These channels are meant to be serious and mods/admins will deny individual people the ability to participate in discussions if the channel starts getting abused. I would also like to note that Admin Candidates are not able to promise anything if they get elected as Admin, as we have internal voting for changes to the league/server that requires the majority of admins to vote in favor of a proposed change. If you have any questions regarding anything to do with the Admin Election, ask in here or #help !
