Voting will open shortly after or the next day. There are two admin positions open! The backer pool consisted of 18 former admins, GMs, and Committee Heads. Anexilia (GM), Assass1n (GM), Fuffy (Former Admin), Hawkish (GM), Hermit (GM), Homer (CH - Numbers), JermzPC (GM), megatallicayer (CH - Mods), mutant (CH - TM), Nightfury (CH - Tech), Spence (Former Admin/GM), Splatmonkey (Former Admin), Supreme (GM), TNoodle (Former Admin/GM), VIPVeral (Former Admin), yeldniL (CH - Events), and yetiF (CH - Stats).   • kowalskey
  • King Willy
  • 42Firehawk
  • Apache
  • scraps
  • SmartAsSuperman Please use the threads to ask candidates questions, even if it is the same question for each candidate. These channels are meant to be serious and mods/admins will deny individual people the ability to participate in discussions if the channel starts getting abused. I would also like to note that Admin Candidates are not able to promise anything if they get elected as Admin, as we have internal voting for changes to the league/server that requires the majority of admins to vote in favor of a proposed change. If you have any questions regarding anything to do with the Admin Election, ask in here or #help!
