Why do you want to become an Admin? I have been a part of the league for 4 seasons now and I have been a AGM for 2 of those seasons. I have seen a lot of the inner workings from both a players perspective and more in depth as an AGM and have a good understanding of how the league is run, and what I believe should be improved and/or reworked. ---------- What prior experience do you have that you believe will help you as an Admin? I have been a part of the events, stats, and media committees for multiple seasons as well as previously mentioned having been AGM for OS for 2 seasons. (also I'm totally the best player in the CSC 10mans hub) ---------- What things do you hope to implement, reinforce, or remove as an Admin? I would like to refocus the league a to lean a bit more towards the pure ""fun"" side of our league as opposed to the strictness of other leagues people play in to play (in my opinion) at a higher commitment level. I would like to make an effort to minimize the necessity of 4v5s in matches due to no faut of the GM/players in the server. Playing in a 4v5 hurts the integrity of the league from a competition perspective, and could potentially be the difference in teams making/missing playoffs. Personally I have no fun playing on either side and it leaves a sour taste in players mouths when you are forced to play down a player when its no fault of yours or your organizations. I would also like to work with events committee add more incentivization for people to participate in non-league events. Such as leaderboards and potentially some small prizes for events such as Wingman, Old map, or 1v1 tournaments. With off season being so hectic for upper management (Admins, GMs, etc.) players, both returning and new tend to be left with little to engage with the league before combine start back up again. I am more than happy to answer any and all questions anyone might have and expand on any points I have made already. Thank you all for your time and I look forward to talking to y'all!
